Insights

The complex relationships amongst different elements need to be understood before the data can be used for any other purpose. These relationships as well as interaction between the data elements are typically referred to insights and form the basis of use of data be it for predictive analytics or optimization or for any other purpose. This insight generation process forms the backbone of today’s data driven decision making platforms and our solution provides the capability to mine available data across different functions and generate these insights. Depending upon the industry and the target solution area the techniques involved may be like principle component analysis, factor analysis, clustering, regression etc.

Optimization

Hundreds of decisions are made in today's enterprises by teams spread across geographies. Strategic, tactical and operational decisions are all happening in tandem and drive the planing to executions cycles. The time horizons involved put up a unique challenge in terms of speed and the quality of the decision. In order to handle to all possible scenarios we support both exact methods like integer programming, mixed-integer, integer programming as well as approximate methods (heuristics and meta-heuristics) like Genetic Algorithms, Tabu Search, simulated annealing etc. The exact models to be used are decided on the basis of required balance between quality (optimality) vs. the solution time

Prediction

Predictive modelling offers a peek into the future based on a clear understanding of the past and the present. Our platforms are designed to help discover the data elements and their hidden relationships and use these insights to predict the future events with confidence thereby enabling decision makers to take insights driven decisions. Depending upon the industry and type of the problem being addressed we provide the ability to choose models based on self-learning (machine learning, neural networks) to simple regression.

Expertise

We specialize in techniques such as Factor Analysis, Cluster Analysis, CHAID Analysis, Perceptual Mapping, Discriminant Analysis, Multiple & Logistic Regression, Forecasting & Predictive Modelling, Customer Segmentation, Churn Prediction, Cohort Analysis.

Enterprise Data Governance

MDM institute defines the Data Governance as “the formal orchestration of people, processes and technology to enable an organization to leverage data as an enterprise asset.” We view data governance as a decision rights and an accountability (RACI) framework to enable and encourage in assessment, valuation, management (creations, use, archival and deletion), storage and use of data and information. We provide end to end services to support your data governance journey, right from helping you define, plan, manage and implement your strategy by identifying (and not limited to) data policies, data standards, data issues management, management of data projects and services, quality and data information, recognized value of data

Master Data Management

The underlying objective of the Master Data Management (MDM) to create and maintain consistent and accurate lists of master data. MDM thus may be viewed as the combination of technology, tools, and processes to aforesaid the objective. With our understanding and experience with MDM, we can help you identify, plan, scope and implement MDM into your organization as per your specific needs and requirements. Our MDM services and project plans will be tailored and influenced by your requirements, priorities, resource availability, time frame, and the size of your MDM problems.

Data Warehouse/ETL/Integration

With the almost continuous stream of data being created in the enterprise, the need exists to provide a reliable, responsive, scalable, efficient and cost effective warehousing solution. We provide end to end services on your data and warehousing related needs. We also provide integration and ETL services to enable the applications and systems to interact with each other. Our services are all BIG data ready and complements well with the evolving needs of today’s organizations.
